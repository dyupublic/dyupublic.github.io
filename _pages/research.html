Research interest: I am particularly interested in leveraging text data to better understand individual and community health. I apply natural language processing and machine learning on large-scale health data with a focus on modeling problems and proposing novel algorithms for real-world applications including but not limited to social media and medical data.

Given my passion for teaching and learning, I'm also open to explore research in pedagogy.

Please see here for my published work.

Projects

• Weight Loss Drugs on Reddit: This project examines Reddit discussions related to the use of weight loss drugs. We aim to identify medical information about these drugs to support phenotyping based on user-generated content.
